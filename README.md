## 🛠 Troubleshooting common issues

If you face problems, check these solutions:

- **No sound after installation:** Verify that FxSound is selected as your default audio device in Windows sound settings. Right-click the speaker icon in your taskbar, select Sound Settings, and ensure the playback device matches your hardware.
- **Microphone issues:** FxSound primarily affects audio playback. If your microphone stops working, check the Windows privacy settings to ensure your application has permission to access your microphone hardware.
- **Installer errors:** If the installer fails, restart your computer and disable your antivirus software temporarily. Some security programs prevent new applications from registering audio drivers. Turn your antivirus back on once the installation finishes.
- **Software does not open:** Check the system tray at the bottom right of your screen. The application might be running in the background. Right-click the icon to open the main window.
- **Sound skips or cracks:** Use the task manager to check your CPU usage. High loads on your processor can interrupt the real-time audio processing. Close unused applications to free up system resources.
